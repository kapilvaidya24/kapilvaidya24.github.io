I am currently an applied scientist at AWS, where I work on NL2SQL in Amazon Redshift. I was previously a CS PhD in the MIT [Data Systems Group](http://dsg.csail.mit.edu/), where I work on applying machine learning to database systems. I was advised by [Prof. Tim Kraska](http://people.csail.mit.edu/kraska/) and I closely collaborated with [Prof. Michael Mitzenmacher](https://www.eecs.harvard.edu/~michaelm/) on learned filters. I have previously interned at Microsoft Research with [Anshuman Dutt](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/people/andut/), [Vivek Narasayya](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/people/viveknar/) and [Surajit Chaudhuri](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/people/surajitc/) from the [DMX](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/group/data-management-exploration-and-mining-dmx/) group on learned query optimisation. I completed my bachelors in Computer Science at IIT Bombay where I worked with [Prof. Sudarshan](https://www.cse.iitb.ac.in/~sudarsha/).
